Calendar Help

Using the Calendar Page

The Weekly Calendar page has two sections: the Search section and the Week View. The Week View displays the days of the week from Sunday to Saturday.

If the day you are looking for is already displayed in the weekly calendar view, there is no need to use the search feature. If the day you are looking for is not displayed, you will need to use Search.

Using Search

Today

  • Clicking Today will display today's date in the Week View.

Previous Week

  • Clicking Previous Week will change the week displayed in the Week View to the previous week.

Next Week

  • Clicking Next Week will change the week displayed in the Week View to the next week.

Current Week

  • Clicking Current Week will change the week displayed in the Week View to the current week.

Go To Date

  • Using the Date Picker, select the date you wish to review.
  • Click "Go To Date." This will change the week displayed in the Week View to include the date you entered.

Week View and Day View

Week View

The Week View displays the days of the week from Sunday to Saturday. If the week displayed contains the current day, that day will be highlighted.

At the top of each day's block is that day's date. Inside each day's block are the appointments scheduled for that day. The earliest appointment for the day will appear first, and the latest appointment will appear last. An empty day's block means no appointments are scheduled for that day.

Day View Appointment Block

The Appointment Block contains information about the appointment. Below is a description of each line in the Appointment Block.

  • Top line is the appointment time
  • Client's name
  • Service type
  • Appointment status - Booked / Completed
  • Edit Button - Click to edit or reschedule the appointment
  • Finish Session Button - Click to finish the session, enter payment information, and complete the post-appointment summary notes
  • Cancel Button - Click to cancel the appointment

Entering an Appointment

  • Click on a day's date at the top of the Day View.
  • This will open the Daily Schedule page so that you can enter details for a new appointment.

Edit Appointment

Use Edit to change any part of the appointment, including rescheduling to a different date, changing the time, or changing the service type.

Finish Session

Clicking Finish Session will first take you to the Add Income page/form. Enter the payment information for the appointment and then click Save Income.

After clicking Save Income, you will be taken to the Appointment Summary page so you can enter details of the appointment, such as products used or recommended, at-home care, and notes.

Cancel

Clicking Cancel will allow you to cancel the appointment. After clicking Cancel, you will receive a confirmation prompt before the appointment is canceled.
